I'm Siarhei Baradulia, a QA engineer at JetBrains, currently living in Amsterdam.

Most of my work sits at the intersection of product quality and engineering reality.

That naturally led me to AI testing from two directions: evaluating AI systems, and integrating AI into quality engineering through quality signals, clear evaluation criteria, feedback loops, and structured workflows.

I'm also drawn to vibe coding because it's changing how software gets built right now. When the cost of trying ideas drops, taste, judgment, and verification matter even more. The goal is not just to keep quality from slipping, but to raise the bar.

This blog is where I write about that intersection: quality as an engineering discipline, AI as both a system under test and a tool inside the testing process, and modern software development as something that needs better frameworks, not just faster tools.

Outside of work, I'm enjoying life in Amsterdam and everything that comes with learning a new city slowly and paying attention to the details.
